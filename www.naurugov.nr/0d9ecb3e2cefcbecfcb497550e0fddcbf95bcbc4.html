Elected members for Nauru elections 2016

Nauru went to the polls at the weekend (Saturday 9 July) which saw the return of 13 of the 18 sitting MPs that contested the elections with six new members making their debut.
 
Seven of the eight constituencies cast their vote on 9 July except for Aiwo which had its polling postponed until today (11 Jul).
 
The Electoral Commissioner Joseph Cain declared the following candidates as elected members for their respective constituencies.
 
Yaren
Charmaine Scotty 340.033 and Kieren Keke 219.667.
 
Buada
Shadlog Bernicke 361.750 and Jason Bingham Agir 306.083.
 
Boe
Asterio Appi 248.633 and Baron Waqa 246.134.
 
Anetan/Ewa
Sean Oppenheimer 354.067 and Cyril Buraman 326.399.
 
Anabar/Ijuw/Anibare
Riddell Akua 263.531 and Jaden Dogireiy 254.036.
 
Menen
Tawaki Kam 519.022, Lionel Aingimea 436.223 and Vodrick Detsiogo 337.260.
 
Ubenide
David Adeang 542.361, Ranin Akua 497.212, Russ Kun 467.146 and Valdon Dowiyogo 423.676.
 
Aiwo
Milton Dube 395.567 and Aaron Cook 391.350.
 
Parliament is expected to sit this week.
